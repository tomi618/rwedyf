Our Core Values: Empowering women and children through education, health, economic justice, and stronger communities
Mission Statement: Integrity, Accountability, Human Rights, Transparency, Gender Equity, Team Work, Leadership

(RWYDEF) was founded on October 10, 2002, in Lokoja, Kogi State, as a non-governmental organization committed to impacting lives positively. Our mission is to empower women and the young by providing them with the skills, resources, and support they need to discover and harness their inherent strengths. Through targeted training programs, mentorship opportunities, and community-driven projects, overcoming barriers, and turning their aspirations into tangible achievements. By fostering an environment of encouragement, collaboration, and personal growth, RWYDEF helps individuals build meaningful, purpose-filled lives and contribute positively to the broader society.

We are founded on the principle of compassion—loving others as we do ourselves—recognizing that life presents different challenges to each of us. We believe that true service to humanity goes beyond fulfilling civic responsibilities; it is reflected in our willingness to give back, to lend a helping hand in any way we can, no matter how small.

Our Goals:

  • Introduction and sustenance of a Youth Educational Scheme to provide scholarship and other educational assistance
  • Help develop new systems and work with existing structures to incorporate gender and human rights issues and mechanisms
  • Create awareness in the public on health issues such as HIV and Malaria and provide systems for access to affordable and effective health care
  • Help rural communities to develop and maintain a Micro-Credit Scheme for rural market women and proffer ways for access to capital with training on financial and managerial skills
  • To create grounds for children to express themselves through development of Leadership skills
  • Provide forums for community discussions as a research tool for NEEDS assessment to provide data for policy makers
